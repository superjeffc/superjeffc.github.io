Purple Potato Latte

⏱️ 30 Mins 🥣 Serves 4

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Boil or steam the sweet potatoes until soft.
  2. Once the sweet potatoes have cooled down, remove the skin.
  3. In a blender, add the peeled sweet potatoes, and some milk.
  4. Blend and add more milk as needed, depending on your preference for thickness.
  5. Pour the mixture into a cup and microwave until warm.
  6. Top with a sprinkle of cinnamon.
