Oatmeal Energy Bars

⏱️ 20 Mins 🥣 Serves 16

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. In a large bowl, mash all the bananas until smooth.
  3. Incorporate the peanut butter, mixing until smooth.
  4. Incorporate the cocoa powder, mixing until smooth.
  5. Incorporate the oats, mixing until all the oats are thoroughly covered.
  6. Pour the batter into a baking pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes.
